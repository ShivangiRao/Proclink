Our Company

Proclink consulting was born in 2014 as a division of rapidly growing financial analytics and technology development company having headquarter in US and delivery center in India.

In 2017, Proclink consulting was carved out as a separate company in order to have dedicated management and growth strategies implemented.

Proclink works as long term partners to our clients in Oil and Gas industry for transforming their business and making it nimble to respond to rapidly changing market dynamics.

Our business is organized in two division – Solutions and Services, Innovation and Product Development to serve Oil and Gas Industries, City development authorities and Public services
